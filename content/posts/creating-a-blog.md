### Why I created a blog.
Creating a blog has been on my todo list for the past 6 years of my life easily. Today I finally
got around to checking it off of my evergrowing list. My primary reasoning was that I realized
about half of the work I do related to software engineering has no sort of public reflection.
Over the course of my "career" I've worked on numerous projects that have not fully blossomed
or been seen by the public. A blog will allow for me to reflect on and showcase various projects
that I have worked on, while also serving as a project of it's own.
